About Us:

Order Hut was founded in 1967 by Mirzokhid Ganiev. It first started off as a small cafe in downtown New York, called "Hut Cafe".
By the year 1987 the company had already over 30 cafes around United States.
In 2004 when the company reached its peak, Hut Cafe moved to the online world and started working for delivery only which tripled its
income.
